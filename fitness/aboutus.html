About Us

Whatever wellness goal you are fighting for, the team at Body Shot is in your corner. It is our mission to help our people “win their fight” by making fitness as much fun as possible. Body Shot Fitness is a great option for those that find the usual gym experience boring or hard to stick to, or for those who want to add some fun and intense variation to their routine.

We provide expert boxing and fight conditioning (circuit training) fitness classes, with personal training options available. Consistency is the key factor in winning any health and fitness fight. Give us a try, we’re your best shot.

Body Gym Fitness equipment was founded in 1996, in New Delhi. Initially we started with manufacturing and import of domestic fitness equipment, today we have a large range of fitness equipment, both indoor and outdoor, including exercise bikes, home gyms, cross trainers, benches, and many more. We serve all major cities of India with utmost customer value and continuous support.

Not just bikes and benches, we have also extended our production lines to making the Outdoor Fitness Equipment. A whole range of fitness equipment known by the names of outdoor fitness, green gym, park fitness etc…, setup in parks and open grounds is a great idea to promote fitness among families and different age groups. Also being beneficial and cost effective, these machines are not only meant for teenagers or adults but for an age group of 10 year – 90 years, which includes children, woman and senior citizen too.
