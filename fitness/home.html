About Us

Whatever wellness goal you are fighting for, the team at Body Shot is in your corner. It is our mission to help our people “win their fight” by making fitness as much fun as possible. Body Shot Fitness is a great option for those that find the usual gym experience boring or hard to stick to, or for those who want to add some fun and intense variation to their routine.

We provide expert boxing and fight conditioning (circuit training) fitness classes, with personal training options available. Consistency is the key factor in winning any health and fitness fight. Give us a try, we’re your best shot.

Zumba

View Details

Zumba combines Latin dance, hip-hop, martial arts, and resistance training in an upbeat fitness choreography that makes working out tons of fun. A typical, 60-minute class helps relieve stress, tone muscles, and burn calories. Appropriate for all ages and fitness levels, it’s no wonder that this confidence-boosting, rhythmic workout is such a hit.

Bikram Yoga (Hot Yoga)

08:00 AM - 10:00 AM

View Details

The 90-minute yoga class includes 26 hatha yoga postures and two pranayama breathing exercises. Commonly referred to as "hot yoga," Bikram is performed in a room heated to 105 degrees to ease the body into its most flexible state. Challenging the mind and body alike, this form of yoga focuses on the importance of oxygen flow and breathing.

Pilates

08:00 AM - 10:00 AM

View Details

This low-impact, conditioning workout is filled with body-sculpting moves that only require a space the size of an exercise mat. Instructors help participants connect the mind and body through a series of small, breath-controlled movements that help build flexibility, muscle strength, and endurance in the legs, abs, arms, hips, and back.
